## **<ins>Principle of mathematical induction:</ins>** 

Suppose that one day a genie grants Amal three wishes. Amal makes two wishes and then for his third wish, Amal wishes for three more wishes the next day. Given the fact that Amal can always use his third wish to renew his wishes for the next day, one can prove that from that first day onward, Amal can have three wishes every day for the rest of his life. This sequence of implications is an example of the principle of mathematical induction ( induction for short).

# Principle of mathematical induction:
